Basic skipper certificate

540$ per person.
30 hours
For those who are new to sailing or have some sailing experience. The aim of the course: to acquire the necessary skills to be able to sail safely, as skipper or crew member, on a 6 to 10 meters sailboat. Opportunity to pass the Sailing Canada exam to obtain the patent.
More details about the course

Skills you will develop

  • Man overboard procedure
  • Anchorage
  • Docking and casting off
  • Marine weather
  • Reading nautical maps
  • Reefing
  • Sailing priority
  • Adjusting the sails

regatta

135$ per person.
5 evenings
regatta is a race between multiple boats. This course will allow you to acquire the skills to maximize the speed of your boat, notably by using the spinnaker.

Skills you will develop

  • Iron the spi in the bag and avoid the spin
  • Place the spinnaker bag on the foredeck
  • Swing the pole to the right height
  • Hoist the spinnaker and adjust it to give the best lift
  • Gybing without the spinnaker emptying and adequate position of the crew
  • Lower the spinnaker efficiently to be re-hoisted quickly
